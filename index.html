About Google AdSense

Google AdSense is a program that allows website publishers to earn money by displaying targeted advertisements on their sites. By placing a small piece of code on your pages, Google serves ads relevant to your content and audience.

Getting Started with Ads

Once your account is approved and your site is connected, ads will start appearing automatically if you have "Auto Ads" enabled. Alternatively, you can create specific ad units to place exactly where you want them.
